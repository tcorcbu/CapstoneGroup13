Sheltered

Rafters above our heads and in our minds
We are responsible for what comes next
Bright and burning with a fierce passion to change the world
Yet our perception is not perfect
We are searching for meaning, for answers, for questions
Once we rip off the veneer, we are enlightened
A piece of the universe is uncovered for us
They are just like us, could be us, why aren’t they us?
We want for nothing, they just want something
When things are invisible there is no blame for inaction
We cannot unsee what has been revealed to us
All that’s left to do is show others

Invisible

If we can’t see it
Most people don’t believe it
But it does exist
